Resources

Navigating College as a First-Gen Student is a video in which Daniela Padilla from the Early Academic Outreach Program (EAOP) at UC San Diego shares tips for first-generation college students, including navigating the transition from high school to college life, how to advocate for yourself, how to find community, and more (12:38).

Finaicial Aid GuideBook is a video that help first-time students with their financial aid availability. Here you will explain more about financial aid and steps towards completing your first application.

Developing a Growth Mindset is a Miniclip featuring tips on how students can switch on their growth mindset (4:51).

Success in Life is a video that showcases how to create your own academic goals. Having goals in college is an important part of college. Using methods like SMART goals you will be able to visualize your success in college.

Utilizing College Resources is a YouTube video that talks about counseling options, student health services, fitness choices, academic health, and more (12:21).
